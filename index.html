About Me

I'm Okafor John, a passionate Nigerian designer and developer with over 5 years of experience creating digital experiences that users love. Based in Nigeria, I bring a unique perspective that combines African design aesthetics with modern technological solutions.

My journey began in graphic design, where I developed a keen eye for aesthetics and user-centered design principles. Over the years, I've expanded my skills to include frontend development, allowing me to not only design beautiful interfaces but also bring them to life with clean, efficient code.

I believe that the best digital products are born from the perfect marriage of form and function. When I'm not coding or designing, you can find me exploring new technologies, contributing to open-source projects, or working on personal projects that showcase the beauty of African design in the digital space.
